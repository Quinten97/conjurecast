About DnD Americana

DnD Americana is a live-play dungeons and dragons podcast loosely inspired by American Folklore and history. Join us on an adventure into the 8 Wild Territories as our heros take up the fight against "The Great Darkness". Along the way they meet key npcs some of which are based on Historical or Fictional Americans and team up to settle issues both big and small.
