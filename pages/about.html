About the Podcast

Started in 2023 ConjureCast is an actual-play D&D podcast hosted by Quinten, Hannah and Zach Favorite as well as Nash Blount, Alex Younts and Cory Guion.

The podcast touts their flagship campaign DnD Americana, a campaign inspired by Americana Folklore, and up and coming campaigns such as Horizon's Edge, a SpellJammer setting inspired by Star Trek.

If you'd like to get into contact you can reach us here to book a live show or an ad read.

About our hosts

Quinten Favorite

Quinten Favorite serves as the editor and Dungeon Master for our podcast. With a passion for tabletop gaming that began in 2011, Quinten brings a wealth of experience and creativity to the table. In addition to his role in the podcast, he works as an IT professional, merging his technical skills with his love for storytelling. His diverse hobbies range from opera to woodworking, showcasing his eclectic interests. Interestingly, Quinten also has a unique quirk—he does not possess the physical ability to burp!
