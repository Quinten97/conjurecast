About the Podcast

Started in 2023 ConjureCast is an actual-play D&D podcast hosted by Quinten, Hannah and Zach Favorite as well as Nash Blount, Alex Younts and Cory Guion.

The podcast touts their flagship campaign DnD Americana, a campaign inspired by Americana Folklore, and up and coming campaigns such as Horizon's Edge, a SpellJammer setting inspired by Star Trek.

If you'd like to get into contact you can reach us here to book a live show or an ad read.

About our hosts

Quinten Favorite

Quinten Favorite has been at the helm of the group since 2011, known for his charming and mild-mannered nature. As the eternal Dungeon Master, Quinten thrives on crafting story-rich campaigns with light tones that create memorable experiences for his friends and family. In addition to his DM role, he acts as the media editor for the podcast, embracing the "jack of all trades, master of none" approach. Fun fact: Quinten is a lightweight when it comes to alcohol and can often be found getting tipsy after just a couple of hard apple ciders.


"Ask me again when I'm drunk and I might let you roll for it."

Cliff Emberheart

Alex Younts

Alexander Younts has been a cherished member of the group since 2016, bringing a warm and goofy energy that lights up every session. An IT professional by day and a dedicated roleplayer by night, Alex is known for crafting unique characters with intricate backstories and memorable quirks. His passion for roleplaying shines through as he effortlessly steps into any role, adding depth and unpredictability to the campaign. Though his contributions are centered on playing, his creativity and humor are indispensable to the podcast. Alex is also full of surprises—he proudly sports a third nipple, which, like his characters, is one of a kind.

Cory Guion

Cory Guion has been a vital part of the group since 2013, originally joining as a guest in the first campaign and now playing a key role in Horizons Edge. Known for his intelligence and knack for strategy, Cory excels at creating calculative characters with dynamic personalities and distinct voices that bring depth to the story. Beyond the table, Cory is the walking embodiment of a light encyclopedia, always serving as a fount of knowledge for the group. His thoughtful approach to both gameplay and storytelling ensures that every session is as engaging as it is memorable.

Hannah Favorite

Hannah Favorite has been captivating audiences and fellow players alike since joining the group in 2018. Confident and mysterious, she brings a unique energy to the table, consistently creating characters that challenge her as a player and elevate every campaign she’s part of. In addition to her role as a player, Hannah handles most of the graphic design work for the podcast, adding a touch of artistry both in-game and behind the scenes. Fun fact: Hannah has piebaldism, which adds a striking and beautiful dimension to her complexion and hair, making her as unforgettable as the characters she plays.
